# Boss Encounter: The Great Island-Eater

**Inspiration:** Bismarck (FFXIV), Philippine Folklore (Kulam).
**Setting:** A recently acquired flying island.
**Goal:** Defend the island from a monster rumored to swallow entire landmasses.

--

## 🌪️ Environmental Mechanics

### The Tilting Island
The monster doesn't just bite; it rams. 
* **Ramming Check:** Results based on the chosen stability tactic (e.g. Konstitution, Widerstehen, Fußarbeit or Agilität, Wissen(Gebirge), Akrobatik).
* **14+:** Unaffected.
* **8-13:** Slide towards the edge.
* **5-7:** Slide towards the edge twice.
* **1-4:** Slide towards the edge twice and be stunned.
* **The Edge:** Every player is 5 slides from going overboard.
* **Recovery:** 
    - Undoing one slide requires 2 turns of movement while doing other stuff (0.5 restore per turn).
    - One turn spent exclusively on getting back to safety: Result 8+ restores 1; Result 5-7 restores 0.5.

## 🔮 The Kulam Support Group

Five Kulam have been sent to support the party. While they are masters of sympathetic magic, they are visibly out of their depth against a beast that acknowledges no human laws or contracts. They focus on binding the monster physically and spiritually.

| **Datu Magat (m)**      | Fesseln / Tesselation      | Friendly/Professional, learning what he can while keeping his opinions to himself |

**Specialty Explanations:**
*   **Blood-Sympathetik:** Establishes magical links using harvested blood to ensure the monster is "anchored" to the island's presence.
*   **Fesseln / Tesselation:** Uses spiritual architecture to manifest chains and inescapable bonds.
*   **Anito-Medium:** Acts as the diplomat between the party and the *Anito* (spirits) of the wind and rain.
*   **Land-Connection / Anatomie:** Deep understanding of the island's "body" and how to stitch its spiritual wounds.
*   **Reflexion:** Focuses on turning the kinetic force of the monster's ramming back upon its own hide.

### Kulam Rites (Support Actions)
* **Spectral Tethers:** Datu Magat and Dayang Hiraya use the monster's blood to manifest chains to immobilize the beast/pull it close.
* **Heart of the Island:** Hara Urduja uses the island's soil and a drop of her own blood to create a sympathetic link between the players and the bedrock. This anchors the players, granting a **Bonus Die** to stability checks for a duration.
* **Anito-Call:** Baylan Tala summons ancestral spirits (Anito) to clear a weather effect for 2 rounds.

## 🗺️ The Battle Zones (4 Locations)

1. **The Granite Spire:** The highest peak. 
    - *Effect:* Best for Ranged/Scrying. Includes "The Slippery Steps" (a mossy area leading to the edge where the monster "docks").
2. **The Rainbow Lagoon:** A large rainwater basin.
    - *Effect:* Players can wash off "Leech Bile" or "Barnacle Slime" here to remove debuffs instantly. Safe from "Hurricane Vortex" suction.
3. **The Hanging Mangroves:** Dense trees overhanging the void (includes Cloud-Oaks).
    - *Effect:* Grant a **Bonus Die** to stability checks (easy to grab roots). Barnacles are dense here.
4. **The Sunken Sky-Cave:** A hollow beneath the spire.
    - *Effect:* Safe from Weather (Fog/Wind/Downpour).
    - *Risk:* Attractive to enemies; Sky-Leeches and Mites swarm here to avoid the wind.

## ⚔️ The Encounter Phases

### Phase 1: The Blood Harvest (1-2 Hours)
* **Objective:** Obtain the monster's blood so the Kulam can "Lock On" and immobilize it to stop the ramming.
* **The Pacing:** The monster rams the island every few "beats" (approx. 20 mins). 
* **The Harvest:** Ragin must use *Schattenschritt* to reach the monster during a close pass.

### Phase 2: The Binding & Protection (1 Hour)
* **Objective:** Protect the Kulam while they channel the immobilization ritual.
* **The Tension:** The monster tries to "tug" the island away. The island tilts permanently (1-2 slides toward the edge for everyone).

### Phase 3: The Maw (Final Minutes)
* **The DPS Check:** The tethers snap. The monster tries to swallow the island whole.
* **The Finisher:** Players must strike the uvula. Kobalt's lightning or Cybi's illusions provide the opening.

## 🦠 Parasites & Strategy

1. **Cloud-Barnacles:**
    - *Behavior:* They fly in and thud onto the island. After 1 round, they "harden" into the stone.
    - *Strategy:* While "soft," they can be pried off easily (Praxis/Kraft). Once hardened, they require heat or heavy impact. They spit "Slick Bile" that causes immediate slide checks.
2. **Sky-Leeches (Eels):**
    - *Behavior:* They pounce from the clouds. They latch onto limbs to weight players down (-1 to Agi-checks) or drain mana.
    - *Strategy:* They hate the Lagoon water. Pushing them toward the center or using salt-based attacks works best.
3. **Spore-Mites:**
    - *Behavior:* They cluster in chains. 
    - *Strategy:* Killing one triggers a chain explosion. Players can use "Wind" or "Push" to maneuver the chain toward the monster's mouth before detonating the lead mite.
4. **Vile Mistletoe:**
    - *Behavior:* Lands in the Mangroves and grows rapidly.
    - *Strategy:* If not weeded out (Suchen/Naturkunde), it releases spores that increase the Storm track every 3 rounds.

## 📦 Possible Loot
* **Storm-Pebble:** A vibrating stone for powering flying artifacts.
* **Whalebone Shards:** Can be crafted into ultra-light armor (Agi bonus).
* **Cloud-Silk Glands:** For weaving unbreakable ropes or nets.
* **Indigestible Essence:** A crystalline residue used for high-level magic crafting.
* **Sky-Whale Ambergris:** Highly valuable alchemical base.
* **Maw-Tusk:** Potential for forging a "Giant-Slaying" weapon.

--

## ⚠️ Possible Consequences
* **Lagoon Salination:** The fresh water is ruined, killing the island's rare plants.
* **Altitude Decay:** The island flies lower, entering dangerous "Ground-Mist" territory.
* **The Scarred Spire:** The Granite Spire is partially bitten, losing scrying vantage.
* **NPC Loss:** A specific worker or friend is swallowed/injured during the Maw phase.
* **Anito Fear:** The island's spirits become hostile to the party for "failing" to protect them.
* **Structural Cracks:** The island requires expensive repairs to stop it from drifting into storms.

--

## 📅 Rough Battle Plan
1.  **Arrival (20m):** Monster rams once. 1-2 Barnacles land. Players identify the threat.
2.  **The Hunt (1h):** 2-3 rams. Leeches swarm the Spire. Ragin attempts the blood harvest.
3.  **The Binding (1h):** Kulam start the ritual. Mistletoe starts growing in the Mangroves. Constant Agi-checks to stay upright.
4.  **The Maw (Last Stand):** The beast opens wide. 3 Rounds to deal enough "Impact" to force a retreat.
